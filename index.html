Registered Valuation

IBBI Registered Valuer

🏅 Securities & Financial Assets · IBBI Registered

Mohit Sagar holds registration as a Valuer under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India (IBBI) for the asset class of Securities and Financial Assets. Our valuations follow internationally accepted methodologies — DCF, Comparable Multiples, NAV — and are prepared in strict conformance with IBBI, SEBI, Companies Act, and FEMA requirements.
